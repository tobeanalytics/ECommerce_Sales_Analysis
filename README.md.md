# E-Commerce Data Analysis Project

## Analysis Goal
Analyze raw e-commerce transaction data to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.  
Identify patterns to optimize revenue, product offerings, and customer segments.

## Objectives
1. *Data Cleaning*
   - Remove duplicates, handle missing CustomerIDs and product descriptions.
   - Standardize text columns and ensure data consistency.

2. *Feature Creation*
   - Calculate TotalPrice = Quantity Ã— UnitPrice.
   - Extract Month/Year from InvoiceDate.
   - Segment customers (high-value vs regular).

3. *Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)*
   - Identify top-selling products.
   - Analyze total sales by country.
   - Track monthly revenue trends.

4. *Visualization*
   - Bar charts for top products and countries.
   - Line charts for monthly revenue trends.

5. *Insights & Recommendations*
   - Highlight profitable products and customer segments.
   - Suggest business growth opportunities based on observed patterns.
